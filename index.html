Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about SuccessPath and how we can help you after your A/L results.

Ask Us Directly →
Is the first mentorship session really free? ▼
Yes, completely free. Your first session with a SuccessPath mentor has no cost. We believe every student deserves guidance, and we want you to experience the value before committing to any programme.
My A/L results were not great — can you still help? ▼
Absolutely. SuccessPath specialises in helping students with all ranges of results. There are always multiple pathways available — NCOE, SLIATE, OUSL, external degrees, vocational training, and more. A lower Z-score does not mean a closed door.
How do sessions work — online or in person? ▼
We offer both. Students across Sri Lanka can join via video call, and in-person sessions are available at our centres. Most students start online and find it just as effective as face-to-face mentoring.
Which A/L streams do you cover? ▼
All four streams — Physical Science, Biological Science, Commerce, and Arts. Our mentor pool includes graduates and professionals from every stream, so you'll always be matched with someone who understands your path.
Do you help with overseas university applications? ▼
Yes. We guide students through scholarship applications, TNE (transnational education) partnerships, and direct overseas admissions — including UK, Australia, India, and Malaysia pathways.
